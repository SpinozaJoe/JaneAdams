WORKSHOPS

I run nutritional workshop throughout the year on a variety of topics including skin health, weight loss and management, boosting immunity and digestion.

These workshops are held from my home clinic (12 Stuart Green, EH12 8YF) and are limited to 8 people. They run for approximately 1.5 hours and you will receive handouts with information to take away. (This way you can focus on listening and not have to take notes).

Generally, the format is around 1 hour information session with a chance to ask questions and have some refreshments afterwards.

Prices will range between £10-20 depending on content, length of duration and handouts/freebies given out.
